I built an anime analytics dashboard using Python and Plotly on a dataset of 18,000+ anime. 
I used Pandas for data cleaning, Plotly for interactive visualizations, 
and integrated Groq's LLaMA3 to generate AI-powered industry insights dynamically.


I used Pandas to clean 18,000 rows of anime data — extracted years from date strings, handled missing values, and exploded genre lists for accurate counting. I built 5 interactive Plotly charts and integrated Groq LLaMA3 to generate dynamic insights based on calculated statistics from the dataset.
